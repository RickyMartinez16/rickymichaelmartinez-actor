Ricky comes from the vibrant shores of New Jersey where being a beach kid meant pranking the tourists and eating ice cream for every meal. Where the sound of breaking glass and fireworks going off is nothing to be surprised about, but to be embraced.

He is a graduate from the prestigious William Esper Studio's Two Year Meisner Training Program. He is also a current Advanced Level Improvisor at the Upright Citizen's Brigade and is a graduate of Rutgers University.

When not acting you can catch Ricky Co-Hosting a fantasy football podcast, re-reading Harry Potter or playing soccer somewhere. Recently moved to Atlanta and with a home still in New Jersey and contacts in LA, the ability to work coast to coast has never been higher

"You miss 100% of the shots you don't take - Wayne Gretzky"
